About Me.

Tim Hortons is a popular Canadian fast-food chain known for its coffee, donuts, and casual dining. Founded in 1964 by hockey player Tim Horton and Jim Charade in Hamilton, Ontario, it has become a cultural icon in Canada.

  • The restaurant offers a variety of breakfast items, sandwiches, and baked goods. With thousands of locations worldwide, Tim Hortons is a beloved destination for quick, affordable, and tasty meals.
